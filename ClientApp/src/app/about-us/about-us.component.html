AnalySim

AnalySim is a data sharing platform that seek to simplify the visualization of dataset. Collaborate together and host your data to the world, or browser through our multiple user generated project

Our Aim

We aim to support the scientific community by providing collaboration tools for analyzing large-scale datasets with many parameter dimensions. This project started in 2016.
